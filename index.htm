  • Our Mission

    We are committed to bettering the lives of Single Mothers and their children, by creating a foundation of independence through advocacy, the offering of resources, and mental health support.

  • Our Values

    Compassion. Community. Service. Respect. Equality.

    We value the well-being of others. We recognize and appreciate our community and strive to give back. We aim to address the public’s needs and value the opportunity to be a positive solution for change. We admire the differences and individuality of each person's strengths, weaknesses, and perspectives. We accept all. All are welcome. All are loved.

    Our Impact
 
 

Get the help YOU need

The Single Mothers Project specifically helps working single mothers who make too much for government assistance, but not enough to thrive. We offer financial assistance for day-to-day expenses like utilities, rent payments, groceries and child care expenses.

Once we offer financial assistance, we offer a variety of resources, to help single mothers better their financial, personal, and emotional well-being.

2022 Donation Initiatives

Check out our Initiatives of 2022 and Donate to the cause that speaks to you!

  • Vehicles for Single Mothers

    To many of our mothers, a vehicle is a luxury. A Single Mothers’s life can be hard enough juggling work schedules, daycare, after care, doctor appointments, grocery shopping and so much more. A safe, reliable vehicle can make all the difference in the lives of single mothers across our community. We’d like to start this initiative to give these families every chance at a better life, higher success rates, and peace and harmony in their lives.

    DONATE

  • Education

    Part of The Single Mothers Projects' mission is to help single mothers gain independence and better their lifestyle, making them capable of providing the best possible living situation for their children. In addition, children growing up in single-mothers families typically do not have the same economic resources as those growing up in two-mothers families. Compared with children in married-couple families, children raised in single-mothers households are more likely to drop out of school. By donating to this initiative, you will provide single mothers or their children with scholarships, helping them achieve their independence goals.

    DONATE

  • Extra Curricular Activities

    We understand the importance of children being involved in extracurricular activities. We want to ensure that a child raised by a single mothers never misses an opportunity to participate for financial reasons. From football camps and registration to Ballet, we can provide the funding for single mothers' children to follow their dreams with your contribution to this initiative!

    DONATE

  • Community Space Build

    For The Single Mothers Project to be successful, we need sufficient space to hold all we have envisioned for the organization to run smoothly. By donating to this initiative, you will help us find our dream location, which will allow us to hold our support groups, provide counseling services, hold life skill educational workshops, and serve our clients with the care they deserve, all in one comfortable and welcoming place!

    DONATE
